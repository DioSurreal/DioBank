## Baseline Rules

-Customer can own multiple accounts
-Account must belong to exactly one customer
-Transfer amount must be > 0
-Every transfer must create:

    1 debit
    1 credit

-Ledger entries are immutable

-KYC must be approved before account opening

-Cheque must be validated before clearing

-Loan requires fraud check
